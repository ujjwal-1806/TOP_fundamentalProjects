What are Party Pinwheels?

These colorful pinwheels feature creamy veggie filling for a party-ready and eye-catching appetizer.

Home cooks love the crowd-pleasing flavor, making it an easy go-to for gatherings. "Everyone expects them at a party," Allrecipes member debgrin says. "Easy and delicious!"

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 4 (12 inch) flour tortillas
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • 1 (2 ounce) can slice black olives

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2
    Mix together cream cheese, ranch dreassing mix, and green onions in a medium bowl until combined.
  3. Step 3
    Spread onto each tortilla. Sprinkle evenly with cheese, red pepper, celery, and olives.
  4. Step 4
    Roll up tortillas, then wrap each one tightly in aluminium foil.
  5. Step 5
    Chill in the refrigerator, 2 hours to overnight.
  6. Step 6To serve, unwrap each rolled tortilla; trim ends and slice into 1-inch pieces.
  7. Step 7
    Enjoy!
