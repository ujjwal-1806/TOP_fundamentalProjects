What is Sausage Balls?

These sausage balls are a real crowd pleaser, whether you serve them as a breakfast or brunch treat, a holiday party appetizer, or a game-day nibble. Savory sausage, store-bought biscuit mix, and sharp Cheddar cheese team up for a yummy savory flavor that can't be beaten! My family makes these every Christmas morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage, at room temperature
  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 12 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Gather all Ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  2. Step 2
    Combine room temperature sausage, biscuit mix, and shredded Cheddar together in a large bowl; mix with your hands until well combined. The mixture may seem dry at first, but keep mixing until it comes together evenly.
  3. Step 3
    Roll mixture into about 30 walnut-sized balls; transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Step 4
    Bake in the preheat oven until golden brown and sausage is cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Step 5
    Enjoy!
